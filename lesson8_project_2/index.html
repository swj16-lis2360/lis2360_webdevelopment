Types of Rocket League Players The Try-Hard

These players only care about climbing the ranks and becoming one of the best. They don't care about the fancy decals or shiny goal explosions. All they want to do is score goals and win games.

Types of Rocket League Players The Fashionista

Who needs a good ranking when you can make your car look like a spaceship? These players spend more time in the car custimization menu than in actual games. They hoard items and hoard even more car designs.

Types of Rocket League Players The Casual

Amongst most of the playerbase, there are three cars that dominate the meta. The Casual player couldn't be bothered with such a thing. Want to use the proteus because it has small arms? Go for it. Just got a viking helmet? Equip it.
